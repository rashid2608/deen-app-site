Overview

Deen ("the App") is a free, non-commercial Islamic application built as sadaqah jariyah (ongoing charity). Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This policy explains what data is accessed, how it is used, and your rights.

In short: we do not collect, store, or share any personal data. The only exception is anonymous crash reports (described below) to help us fix bugs and improve stability.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No account registration or login required
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No browsing or search history
  • No contacts, photos, or files from your device

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server:

  • Reading progress — your last-read position in the Quran
  • Bookmarks — saved verses and content
  • Preferences — theme, translation choice, reciter selection, dhikr counters
  • Cached content — downloaded Quran audio and mushaf images for offline use

Location Data

The App requests access to your device location solely to provide:

  • Accurate prayer times based on your geographic coordinates
  • Qibla compass direction to the Ka'bah

Location data is processed on-device and sent only to the AlAdhan API (aladhan.com) as latitude/longitude coordinates to calculate prayer times. No personally identifiable information is attached to these requests. Location data is never stored on any server.

Third-Party Services

The App communicates with the following external services to provide its functionality:

  • Quran.com API (quran.com) — to fetch Quran text, translations, and audio recitations.
  • AlAdhan API (aladhan.com) — to calculate prayer times and convert Hijri dates.
  • Hadith API (fawazahmed0) — to fetch hadith collections.
  • Sentry (sentry.io) — to receive anonymous crash and error reports for stability improvements.

These services receive only the data necessary for their function (e.g., coordinates for prayer times, chapter number for Quran text). No personal or identifying information is sent. Please refer to each service's own privacy policy for how they handle requests.

Crash & Error Reporting

The App uses Sentry (sentry.io) to collect anonymous crash and error reports. When the App encounters an unexpected error, the following non-personal, technical data may be sent to Sentry:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version and build number
  • Error stack traces (technical details about what went wrong)
  • General device state (memory usage, battery level)

This data is fully anonymous — it contains no personal information, no location data, no user identifiers, and no content you have viewed or interacted with. It is used solely to identify and fix bugs that affect app stability. Crash data is retained by Sentry for 90 days and then automatically deleted. You can review Sentry's privacy policy at sentry.io/privacy.

Notifications

The App may send local notifications for prayer time reminders (adhan). These are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any external push notification service. No notification data is sent to any server.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected at all, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Advertisements & Monetisation

The App contains no advertisements, no in-app purchases, and no subscription models. Voluntary donations to support development are accepted through external platforms (Buy Me a Coffee, PayPal) and are entirely optional.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on any server, there is no risk of server-side data breaches. All user preferences and cached content remain on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the App or clearing its data through your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
